Ranger Attack 15Trick Shot

You take several chaotic shots, which strike various parts of your foes’ bodies, inhibiting the creatures in different ways.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Target: One, two, or three creatures

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.

Effect: Roll a d4 to determine the effect of each attack.
1—You knock the target prone.
2—The target is slowed (save ends).
3—The target is dazed (save ends).
4—The target is immobilized (save ends).


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 40.
