Hethralga was a hag originally in service to Cachlain. She specialized in the formorian grafting techniques, splicing physical and magical abilities from one creature to another. As part of Cachlain's coven, she and he fellow hags kidnapped children of the fey, turned them into formor, and trained them as warriors and slaves of Cachlain. Hethralga betrayed Cachlain and began to work in service of his advisor Sangwyr instead, building Sangwyr his own personal army. When Sangwyr betrayed Cachlain to become a rival warlord, Hethralga went with him.

During her tenure as a double-agent for Sangwyr while working for Cachlain, Hethralga kidnapped Kitara as a child, replaced her eye and trained her in her arcane powers. Kitara escaped Hethralga's coven as a teenager before she completed her training and could be put to service for Sangwyr.
