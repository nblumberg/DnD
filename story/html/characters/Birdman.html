An aged human male who was locked in a cell in the Karak mines. His lined face sported a snow-white beard to match his fringe of hair. He wore the garb of a simple desert peasant, and appeared to have not been there long. Seven yellow canaries perched on his shoulders and hopped around his cell. The Birdman whistled and chirped at his tiny charges. When spoken to, he simply nodded and smiled.

He offered no resistance and allowed himself to be led about. He accepted food and water, smiling and nodding when spoken to but otherwise ignoring the party.

After a night's rest our heroes woke to find the Birdman gone, tracks and yellow feathers leading out into a sandstorm.
