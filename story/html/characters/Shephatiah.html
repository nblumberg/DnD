"Queen" Shephatia was a giant guardian naga ruling over the monsters infesting the Karak mines. She and her demonic guards discovered the Karak Lode from a breach to the Elemental Chaos. A vassal of Sarshan's she was mining it and shipping its wealth to him via the Elemental Chaos.

She used a dryad disguised as an eladrin female as her public stand in, controlling a contingent of fey like satyrs, harpies, and hags. She used the Warden to collect tribute from local villages like Dunesend.

Shephatiah was discovered and defeated by our heroes and her followers cleared from the Karak mines.
