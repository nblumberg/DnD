Andor and Toris Scrollstone were sages who specialized in maps and locations. Commonly consulted by explorers into the Underdark and merchants who had long journeys ahead of them, the Scrollstone brothers were the people to consult if one wished to learn more about Sayre's undercity. They can talk about Auger and its destruction at unknown hands. They have old maps showing what buildings used to be where. Both dwarves were annoyed by the incorrect location of the memorial plaque for Auglos' Tower on Prospect Hill.
